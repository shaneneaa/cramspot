What is this all about?

Cramspot is a platform for finding a workspace that suits to your needs. It is an application which help student to find a place where they can rent a workspace and study area to do their group projects.

Search hundreds of workspaces and studyareas available to find the perfect venue to suit your needs anytime, anywhere.

Beautifully showcase your own studyarea and workspace, market your services to our application, and rent out available rooms, desks, and collaborative spaces. Advertise your venue, increase your drop-in bookings, and attract new clients.
